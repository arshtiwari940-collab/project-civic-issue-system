How can I file a complaint?

You can file a complaint by clicking on the "Register Complain" button and following the instructions.

Click on "Track Complaint" and enter your complaint ID to check the status.

Who can use this portal?

Any resident of Jharkhand can use the Samadhan Portal to raise grievances.
